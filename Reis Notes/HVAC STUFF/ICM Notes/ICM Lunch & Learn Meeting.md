- Surge Protectors:

	- 120/240VAC
	- Cast Metal Enclosure

	- They do offer a 600V surge protector that can cover our Canada friends
	- Could use 600V for all instead of buying multiple variants

- Single Phase Voltage Monitor:
	- Offer a product that has single phase monitoring and surge protection
	- Commonly used on mini splits
	- Focused for products that can't go down
	- They do offer anti short cycle protection on their product
- 3 Phase Voltage Monitoring:
	- We currently buy the ICM401A
	- Use them as a factory installed option
	- ICM450A and ICM450A+ are higher end with more features
	- ICM450A+ offer timestamps and BMS communication capabilities
	- Fun fact they use a Renesas Micro
