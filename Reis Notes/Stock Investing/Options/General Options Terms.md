
- Options Contracts : has an expiration date
- Strike Price: contract price expected for asset within contract period
- Contract Multiplier: each contract is 100 stocks
- Premium: total cost of the options contract or the "deposit fee"
- Bulls: Buy call options 
- Bears: Buy put options
- In the money: when the stock price exceeds the strike price
- Out of the money: when the stock price does not exceed the strike price
- At the money: is when the stock price is equal to the strike price
